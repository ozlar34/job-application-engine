## About Lumen AI

Lumen AI builds an agentic coding platform used by developers to ship software faster. Our community is our distribution: developers discover us through other developers, through the people building in public on our Discord, and through the creators who show what the platform can do. We are AI-native to the core, and we expect everyone here, community included, to build with the tools we ship.

## The role

We are hiring our first **Senior Community Manager** to own the developer community end to end and turn it into a durable growth and product-feedback engine. You will run our multi-platform presence, scale our champions program, and build the loop that carries what developers tell us straight back into the roadmap.

## What you'll own

1. **Developer community ownership.** Own our multi-platform developer community (Discord, Reddit, X, YouTube) end to end: programming, moderation standards, and the day-to-day pulse. Grow it without losing the signal-to-noise that makes a developer community worth being in.

2. **Champions / ambassador program.** Design and run a tier-based champions program: identify the developers and creators already advocating for us, give them a structured program with real benefits, and turn ad-hoc goodwill into a measurable creator pipeline.

3. **Community-to-product feedback loop.** Be the voice of the developer community to our product and engineering teams. Build a repeatable pipeline that turns community signal into structured, traceable insight that product can act on, not a firehose of anecdotes.

## What we're looking for

- 5+ years in community, with senior ownership of a multi-platform community at scale.
- Demonstrated experience building an ambassador, champions, or creator program from scratch.
- A track record of feeding community insight into product decisions in a structured way.
- Fluency with AI tooling in your own workflow. We are AI-native; we want a community lead who builds with AI, not one who only talks about it.
- Strong written communication and the ability to work async across a distributed team.
- Nice to have: experience with developer or technical audiences; comfort with lightweight automation/no-code tooling.

## Logistics

Amsterdam-based hybrid or EU remote. Some travel for community events and meetups.
