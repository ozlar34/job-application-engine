## Usage notes

- **CV summary:** Variant A (Track A roles), Variant D (Track B PM/AI-adjacent roles), or Variant E (Track A adjacent DevRel/Developer Advocate). Trimmed to 2 sentences if header already carries the role title.
- **LinkedIn About:** Variant A or B as paragraph 1; expand into 2–3 supporting paragraphs.
- **Cover letter hook:** Open with sentence 1 of the variant matched to the role; sentence 2 becomes the bridge to why-this-company.
- **Recruiter reply / intro DM:** Variant A, cut sentence 3's "Amsterdam or EU remote" if already established in the thread.
- **"Tell me about yourself":** Speak Variant B or C (Track A), D (Track B), or E (DevRel) out loud — these are the most narrative-friendly openings.
- **Track B quick rule:** Variant D for any PM/Program Manager/AI-adjacent ops title. Never use A/B/C as the base for a Track B application — they bury the PM promotion under community framing.
- **DevRel quick rule:** Variant E for community-first Developer Advocate / DevRel Community Manager roles at no-code/automation or AI-dev-tooling companies. Never use B — it reads as gaming community, not developer community.
