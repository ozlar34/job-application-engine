# Cover Letter Learnings

> Sidecar to `cover-letter-pool.md`, `cover-letter-tailoring-rules.md`, and `cover-letter-voice-profile.md`. Captures manual edits made to skill-tailored cover letter drafts after `/tailor-cover-letter` produced them, classified at capture time, with a manual gate (`/cover-letter-promote-learnings`) for merging durable patterns back into the locked content.
>
> **Synthetic showcase.** The illustrative entries below use the fictional candidate **Alex Rivera** and the fictional target company **Lumen AI** (see `cv-master.md`). Replace them with your own captured edits.
>
> **Active count rule:** entries in `## Active (unpromoted)` classified as `phrasing-preference`, `variant-rule`, `voice-rule`, or `pattern-rule` count toward the threshold. Typo and JD-specific entries do not.
>
> **Threshold:** see `THRESHOLD = 8` in `cover-letter-tailoring-rules.md`. Calibrate after 3-4 applications.
